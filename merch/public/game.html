The Search for La Miel

The Okay Bears left without looking back. They disappeared into the depths of the metaverse and never returned. For a long time, the OkayCubs waited.

But the Cubs learned. They organized. And they discovered something the Bears never found: that moving through space is also moving through time.

Now they travel — era by era — in search of La Miel. The primordial honey that existed in every age of the Earth. Each era holds its own honey, each with unique properties. Each one guarded by those who will not give it up without a fight.

Your journey begins in the Hadean Era — where Magma Honey burns at the core of the world. Defeat its guardians, claim the honey, and travel forward through time.

The honey waits. The guardians do not sleep. Begin the hunt.
