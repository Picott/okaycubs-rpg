Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Introduction

OkayCubs: The Honey Chronicles ("we", "our", "the Service") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

Wallet Address: When you connect your Phantom wallet, we read your public wallet address solely to verify which OkayCubs NFTs you hold. This is a public blockchain address — not personal identifying information.

NFT Holdings: We query the Solana blockchain (via the Tensor API) to identify OkayCubs tokens in your wallet. This data is read-only and not stored on our servers.

Game Progress: Your wins, unlocked eras, and preferences are stored in your browser's local storage. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Order Information: If you purchase merchandise, your shipping address, email, and payment information are processed by our third-party providers (Stripe for card payments, Printful for fulfillment). We do not store payment card details on our servers.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, IP address, or any personal identifying information through the RPG game. We do not use cookies for tracking purposes. We do not access your wallet's private keys, seed phrase, or transaction history beyond NFT ownership verification.

Third-Party Services

Our Service uses the following third-party providers:

Google AdSense: We display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings at adssettings.google.com.

Tensor API: Used to verify NFT ownership on the Solana blockchain and retrieve floor price data. Tensor's privacy policy applies to data processed through their API.

Stripe: Payment processing for merchandise orders. Stripe's privacy policy governs how payment data is handled.

Printful: Print-on-demand fulfillment for merchandise. Shipping addresses provided during checkout are shared with Printful for order delivery.

Vercel: Our hosting provider. Standard server logs (IP addresses, request timestamps) may be collected by Vercel as part of their hosting service.

Data Retention

Game progress data is stored locally in your browser and persists until you clear your browser data. Order information is retained by Stripe and Printful according to their respective retention policies. We do not maintain a user database.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Your Rights

Since we store minimal data and most of it resides on your device or the public blockchain, your control is straightforward: clear your browser's local storage to remove game progress, disconnect your wallet to end the session, and manage ad preferences through Google's Ads Settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out through our community channels or email us at the address listed on our project page.
