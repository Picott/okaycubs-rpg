FAQ

Frequently asked questions

General

What is OkayCubs: The Honey Chronicles?

It's a free-to-play, browser-based RPG built on the Solana blockchain. Players use their OkayCubs NFTs as playable characters to battle through six geological eras in search of primordial honey. Every Cub's traits translate into unique battle stats, making each NFT a one-of-a-kind fighter.

Is it free to play?

Yes. If you hold an OkayCubs NFT in your Phantom wallet, the game is completely free. There are no in-game purchases, tokens to buy, or pay-to-win mechanics. Your NFT is your character — that's all you need.

Can I play without an NFT?

You can try the game in Demo Mode, which provides sample Cubs so you can experience the battle system. Demo progress isn't saved, but it's a great way to see if the game is for you before purchasing an NFT.

Where can I buy an OkayCubs NFT?

OkayCubs are available on Solana NFT marketplaces like Tensor and Magic Eden. Search for "OkayCubs" to find the verified collection. You'll need SOL (Solana's native token) in your Phantom wallet to purchase.

Gameplay

How are my Cub's stats determined?

Stats (HP, ATK, DEF, LCK) are derived from your Cub's NFT traits — fur color, headgear, accessories, background, and more. Rarer traits generally give higher stats. The exact formula is deterministic: the same Cub always has the same stats.

How do I unlock new eras?

By accumulating total victories. You start in the Hadean Era (0 wins needed), and each subsequent era requires more wins: Archean (5), Proterozoic (15), Paleozoic (30), Mesozoic (50), Cenozoic (75). Wins from any era count toward your total.

What happens when my Cub falls in battle?

You can revive your Cub by watching a short ad, which restores them to full HP so you can continue the battle. Alternatively, you can retreat — your Cub heals fully but the battle progress is lost.

Is my progress saved?

Yes. Your total wins, unlocked eras, and progression are saved in your browser's local storage tied to your wallet address. As long as you use the same browser and wallet, your progress persists.

Wallet & Security

Is my wallet safe?

Absolutely. The game only requests read-only access to verify which NFTs you hold. It never asks for transaction approval, never moves tokens, and never accesses your private keys. The wallet connection is purely for NFT verification.

What data do you collect?

We do not collect personal data. The only information the game reads is your wallet's public address and token holdings (to identify your Cubs). No email, no name, no tracking beyond standard web analytics. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Merch Store

How does the merch store work?

Connect your wallet, select a Cub, and choose a product (hoodie, joggers, or cap). The store generates a realistic mockup using Printful's mockup generator — showing your exact Cub on the real product with fabric textures. When you order, Printful prints and ships it directly to you.

Can I pay with crypto?

Yes. The store supports both credit card payments (via Stripe) and Solana Pay. For Solana Pay, you scan a QR code with your Phantom wallet and pay in SOL.

How long does shipping take?

Products are printed on demand by Printful. Production typically takes 2–5 business days, plus shipping time which varies by location. You'll receive tracking information once your item ships.
