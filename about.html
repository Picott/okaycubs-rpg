The Honey Chronicles — In Search of the Golden Drop

The World of OkayCubs

OkayCubs: The Honey Chronicles is an NFT-powered RPG built on the Solana blockchain. Players use their OkayCubs NFTs as playable characters, battling through six geological eras of Earth's history in a quest for La Miel — the primordial honey that has existed since the dawn of time.

Each Cub is unique, with traits that translate directly into battle stats. Fur color, headgear, accessories, and background all influence a Cub's strength, defense, luck, and HP. No two Cubs fight the same way — your NFT is truly your character.

The Lore

"The Okay Bears left without looking back. They disappeared into the depths of the metaverse and never returned. For a long time, the OkayCubs waited."

When the Okay Bears vanished from the Solana ecosystem, the Cubs they left behind were forced to fend for themselves. But these weren't ordinary cubs. They were resilient, resourceful, and bound together by something the Bears never understood: community.

The Cubs discovered that the key to survival wasn't just moving through space — it was moving through time itself. Ancient texts revealed that a substance of immense power had existed in every geological era of Earth: La Miel, the primordial honey. Each era's honey carried unique properties — from the raw destructive force of Hadean magma honey to the time-bending power of Cenozoic frost honey.

Now, the Cubs travel era by era, battling the ancient guardians who protect each era's honey. Every victory brings them closer to assembling the complete collection — and preparing for the day when the Bears might return.

"The Bears may return someday. When they do — you'll be ready."

The Six Eras

The journey through time spans six geological eras, each with its own environment, enemies, honey type, and challenges. Players begin in the Hadean Era and unlock subsequent eras by accumulating victories.

🌋Hadean Era — Magma Honey

The beginning of Earth. Molten rock, constant bombardment, and the first formation of the planet's crust. Magma Honey burns with raw power and grants devastating offensive capabilities. Guardians here are primal and fierce — forged from the planet's molten core. Unlocked from the start; 5 victories to advance.

🪨Archean Era — Stone Honey

The first continents form, the atmosphere thickens. Stone Honey carries the weight of mountains — hardening defenses and granting unshakable endurance. The guardians are ancient, slow but immensely powerful. Requires 15 total victories to unlock.

🌊Proterozoic Era — Tide Honey

Oceans dominate the world, the first complex life stirs beneath the waves. Tide Honey flows with regenerative power — healing wounds and restoring vitality mid-battle. Guardians emerge from the deep, fluid and relentless. Requires 30 total victories.

🌿Paleozoic Era — Bloom Honey

Life explodes across the land. Forests, insects, amphibians — the world pulses with biological energy. Bloom Honey grants lightning speed and venomous strikes. The guardians here are fast, unpredictable, and deadly. Requires 50 total victories.

🦕Mesozoic Era — Titan Honey

The age of giants. Dinosaurs rule the Earth, and only the strongest survive. Titan Honey channels pure brute force — overwhelming enemies with sheer power. The guardians are colossal, each battle a test of endurance. Requires 75 total victories.

❄️Cenozoic Era — Frost Honey

Ice ages reshape the planet. Mammals rise, the world grows cold. Frost Honey bends time itself — slowing enemies and granting glimpses of the future. The final guardians are cunning and ruthless, testing every skill you've earned. Requires 100 total victories.

What Makes OkayCubs Different

Unlike traditional NFT games that require purchasing in-game items or tokens, OkayCubs is completely free to play for all OkayCubs NFT holders. Your NFT is your character — no additional purchases needed. The game verifies wallet ownership on-chain through the Solana blockchain, ensuring only real holders can access the full experience.

The game combines classic RPG mechanics — turn-based combat, stat progression, era-based world exploration — with the unique properties of each NFT. Rarer traits mean stronger stats, but strategy matters more than raw power. Every Cub can make it to the Cenozoic Era with the right approach.

The merch store adds another dimension: players can print their Cubs on real-world products like hoodies, joggers, and caps — with Printful generating realistic product mockups using each player's unique Cub image.
